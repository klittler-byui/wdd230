A Small Town With So Much To Offer!

Litterville is a quaint town full of Southwest charm and eclectic culture. Our downtown district offers a variety of entertainment and dinning. Our beautiful community parks are the center of our town events and gatherings. Just a few miles beyond our borders, are hills and valleys offering a variety of outdoor adventures. Our small town offers everything you need to make it your home!


Explore some of our town features below.

Litterville Demographics

Population

Littlerville has a current population of 25,151 and is growing at a rate of 5.63% annually and its population has increase by 7.345% since the most recent census. Spanning 27 square miles, Littlerville has a population density of 94.5 people per square mile.


The average median household income is $87,234 with a poverty rate of 3.75%. The median age in Litterville is 36.64 years, 35.48 for males, and 37.8 years for females


Racial Compisition

According to the most recent ACS, the racial composition of Littlerville was:

  • White: 57.2%
  • Two or more races: 13.8%
  • Black or African American: 10.1%
  • Native American: 6.50%
  • Asian: 4.3%
  • Other race: 4.35%
  • Native Hawaiian or Pacific Islander: 3.75%
