Our History

Bountiful Foods started as a "senior project" by Kirk Perron, an avid cyclist and healthy-lifestyle advocate, at California Polytechnic State University, San Luis Obispo (Cal Poly). JBountiful Foods was founded on March 31, 1990, by Perron, who opened his first store in San Luis Obispo, California. Kevin Peters, Joe Vergara, and Linda Ozawa Olds, also helped begin the business. It was incorporated in 1990 as Juice Club, Inc. in San Luis Obispo.[2]

 

Our home base moved to our store front in the Carlsbad Village in 1998, and soon the clamour for increased access to our fresh smoothies and juices pushed us to look for new ways to bring our blended goodness to more people. In answer to the growing demand we put our smoothies on wheels and in 2002, the Blue Food truck started making its way along the coast, httting popular beaches in the Huntington area. Not long after, the Yellow and Orange Food Trucks joined the family! Today, our food trucks are an iconic part of beach culture between Carlsbad and San Diego.>

 

For over 30 years now, Bountiful Foods has been blending fresh fruits into healthy drinks, juices, and fruit bowls. We are proud to be an organic and local farm fresh partner in your health.
