Target Audience

The target audience anticipated to visit the Litterville Chamber of Commerce website are small business owners, developers, local citizens, potential home buyers, and tourists. Individuals frequenting the site are likely to be small business owners looking to network and increase their revenue. Tourists looking into local attractions are also likely site visitors.

Persona Small Business Owner
Fictional Name Shandy Johnston
Job Title Business Owener / Baker
Demographics
  • 39 years old
  • Married
  • Mother of two
  • Graduate of Luisiana Culinary Arts Academy
Goals & Tasks

Shandy is focused on growing her dream business. She wants to provide the best pastries and baked goods in the town. Her two teenage children are an integral part of their family-oriented bakery.

Shandy spends her work time:

  • Baking sweet breads and pastries
  • Developing her bakery menu and testing new recipes.
  • Overseeing her 5 employees (including her two children).
  • Running payroll and company accounting.
  • Filming bakery videos and snapshots for her YouTube channel to promote her bakery and increase sales.
  • Marketing her business via social media.
